About

From robotics to trustworthy AI.

I am an AI engineer and researcher with a foundation in robotics. My work sits at the intersection of agentic AI, vision-language models, uncertainty quantification, and embodied intelligence.

I care about systems that do more than produce an answer: they should know when they are uncertain, explain why they acted, and stay dependable outside a clean benchmark.

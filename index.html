§ 01

About

I'm an incoming MS student in AI Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University, coming from a Robotics Engineering background (Honors College, Chongqing University). My work sits at the intersection of embodied AI, uncertainty quantification, and multimodal models — teaching agents to know what they don't know, and to act accordingly.

Recent research spans conformal-prediction-gated navigation for vision-language models, multimodal forgery detection, and evaluation frameworks for frontier MLLMs on spatial reasoning. On the systems side, I've shipped a production-style multi-tool LLM agent and built full-stack autonomous racing software on embedded hardware.

§ 02

Research

W2A

When2Ask: Uncertainty-Aware Interactive Navigation for VLMs in Urban Scenes

Co-First Author · New York University · Advised by Prof. Chen Feng · Oct 2024 – Mar 2025

A conformal-prediction module gates a navigation agent's action policy, escalating to a human only when calibrated uncertainty exceeds a threshold — a +25pp success-rate improvement over relevance/entropy baselines across five cities on the V-IRL platform.

V2V

View2Verify: Forged Image Detection, Localization & Interpretation via VLMs

Undergraduate Thesis · Chongqing University · Advised by Assoc. Prof. Xiangpeng Li · Sep 2025 – May 2026

A dual-stream framework fusing CLIP-ViT semantics with SRM frequency-domain analysis, LoRA-tuned on LLaVA-1.5-7B. 87.16% accuracy on FaceForensics++ (FakeClue), outperforming LLaVA-1.5, InternVL2-8B, and DeepSeek-VL2-Tiny.

SPE

Spatial Performance Evaluation of MLLMs in Outdoor Street Scenes

Research Assistant · New York University · Advised by Prof. Saining Xie & Prof. Chen Feng · Jan – Mar 2025

A standardized evaluation framework benchmarking spatial understanding in GPT-4V, Gemini, and Claude on real street scenes, surfacing failure modes relevant to embodied-agent development.

RRC

RoboRacer Autonomous Racing Stack

UCInspire Program · UC Irvine · Advised by Prof. Yasser Shoukry · Jun – Sep 2025

Full-stack autonomous navigation on Jetson NX: LiDAR-based PID wall-following, Hector-SLAM + EKF localization, and RRT* trajectory planning, validated in F1Tenth Gym before sim-to-real deployment.

BMR

Biomimetic Microrobots for Navigation in Viscous Fluids

Team Lead · Advised by Prof. Zijie Qu (SJTU) · Mar – Aug 2024 · Published, IEEE ICARA 2025

Bacteria-inspired locomotion design for low-Reynolds-number environments, from geometric modeling through fabrication and motion-characterization experiments.
